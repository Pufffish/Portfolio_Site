About Me

I am currently a freshman at NJIT majoring in Human-Computer Interactions. I love this major because it combines computer science, design, and psychology, three fields I love and enjoy doing. I hope to obtain a position as a UX designer, for it is a direct way to see how my work could impact and benefit others in day-to-day life.

I have always loved designing and creating, having extensive experience with Adobe Suite and digital and traditional art. I also enjoy programming, where I see programs come to life. Languages I know include Python, Java, HTML, and CSS.

In my free time, I also like to play video games, I am learning game design and development. My dream would be to have my own studio and game. I also enjoy drawing and designing. I have done yearbook club throughout high school and newspaper club in college, where I get to utilize my graphic design skills.
